Recyclable AHU

A project that I have done during the internship at Arup.
This is a 3D model and prototype of an air-handling unit with plate heat exchanger in the middle.
The real product was prototyped using recyclable materials through lasercutting and 3D printing.
The final product was exhibited in The Building Center in London, UK in September 2016.
